How do you get a security certificate

To get a security certificate, start by choosing a trusted Certificate Authoritywhich includes your domain and company details. Submit the CSR to the CA and complete the domain verification process through email, DNS, or HTTP methods. Once verified, the CA will issue your certificate. Finally, install the certificate on your server and configure it for your website.

how does ssl work

SSL encrypts data between a client and a server, ensuring secure communication. It uses public and private keys for encryption and decryption, authenticated by a trusted certificate authority. This prevents eavesdropping, tampering, and data forgery, establishing a secure channel for sensitive information like passwords or credit card details.

how to stay safe on the internet

Stay safe online by using strong, unique passwords and enabling two-factor authentication. Avoid sharing personal information publicly, clicking suspicious links, or downloading unknown attachments. Use antivirus software, update devices regularly, and secure Wi-Fi networks. Be cautious on social media, verify sources, and think critically to avoid scams and phishing attempts.
