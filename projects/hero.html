My hero- leonardo Dicaprio

Leonardo DiCaprio is my hero because of his incredible range as an actor and his dedication to his craft. From gripping dramas like "The Revenant" to thought-provoking roles in "Wolf of Wall Street" he consistently delivers powerful performances. Beyond acting, his commitment to environmental activism inspires me. He uses his platform to raise awareness about climate change, making him a true role model.

Wolf of Wall street
Jordan Belfort, a stockbroker who rises to immense wealth through fraudulent practices. As he indulges in excess, including drugs and parties, his life spirals out of control, leading to legal troubles and personal downfall.
Shutter island
U.S. Marshal Teddy Daniels, sent to investigate the disappearance of a patient from a mental institution. As he delves deeper, he uncovers disturbing secrets, questioning reality and his own sanity in a tense psychological thriller.
The Revenant
A frontiersman Hugh Glass, who is left for dead after a bear attack. Driven by survival and revenge, he embarks on a grueling journey through harsh wilderness to track down those who betrayed him.
